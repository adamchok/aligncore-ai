# AlignCore Accelerator — Q&A Knowledge Base

## About the Program

**What is AlignCore?**
AlignCore is an AI-native startup accelerator that matches early-stage startups with experienced mentors using intelligent algorithms. We focus on FinTech, HealthTech, EdTech, SaaS, E-Commerce, DeepTech, CleanTech, and other technology sectors.

**What stage of startups do you accept?**
We accept startups from Pre-seed through Growth stage. Most of our cohort companies are at Pre-seed, Seed, or Series A stage.

**What industries does AlignCore focus on?**
Our primary sectors are: FinTech, HealthTech, EdTech, SaaS, E-Commerce, DeepTech, and CleanTech. We also accept exceptional startups in adjacent "Other" categories.

---

## Application & Onboarding

**How do I apply to AlignCore?**
Send us an email at our inbox with a brief description of your company — your name, what you build, the problem you solve, your current stage, and your team size. Our AI will process your application and a team member will follow up within 3 business days.

**What information should I include in my application?**
Include: company name, industry, current funding stage, a one-paragraph description of what you build, the problem you're solving, your team size, and what you're hoping to get from the program.

**Is there an application fee?**
No, applying to AlignCore is completely free.

**How long does the application review take?**
Our AI processes applications immediately. A human team member will follow up within 3 business days to discuss next steps.

**Can international startups apply?**
Yes, we welcome applications from startups worldwide. Mentorship sessions can be conducted remotely.

---

## Mentorship

**How does mentor matching work?**
Our AI analyzes your company profile — industry, stage, problem, and goals — and identifies the best-fit mentors from our network. We consider expertise overlap, industry experience, and mentorship availability.

**How many mentors will I be matched with?**
Typically, each company is matched with 1 primary mentor. Additional mentors may be introduced as the relationship develops.

**How often do mentor sessions happen?**
Session frequency is agreed between the mentor and company. Typically 2–4 sessions per month for active relationships.

**Can I request a specific mentor?**
You can express preferences during onboarding. We'll do our best to accommodate, subject to mentor availability.

**Are mentor sessions paid?**
Mentors in our network volunteer their time as part of the AlignCore community. Sessions are provided at no cost to the startup.

---

## Program Structure

**How long does the program run?**
The core program is 3–6 months, with the option to continue the mentor relationship beyond the formal program period.

**What support does AlignCore provide beyond mentor matching?**
- AI-powered relationship health monitoring
- WhatsApp-based communication tracking
- Knowledge document sharing between mentors and companies
- Activity dashboards and engagement analytics
- Peer company networking within the same industry

**Does AlignCore take equity?**
No. AlignCore does not take equity or charge fees for participation in the standard mentorship program.

## Communication & Logistics

**How will I communicate with my mentor?**
Primary communication is through WhatsApp. We'll set up a dedicated WhatsApp group for you and your mentor, monitored by the AlignCore platform.

**What happens if the mentor relationship isn't a good fit?**
Contact the AlignCore team and we'll facilitate a re-matching process. Your experience and comfort are our priority.

**How do I update my company profile or goals?**
Log into the AlignCore dashboard or reply to this email with updated information. Our team will update your profile accordingly.

**How do I get in touch with the AlignCore team directly?**
Reply to this email or send your query to our inbox. For urgent matters, our team will escalate via WhatsApp.

---

## Technical / Platform

**Is there a startup portal or dashboard?**
The AlignCore platform is managed by our team on your behalf. Relationship health, session notes, and document sharing are handled through our internal dashboard.

**Can I upload documents like pitch decks or financial statements?**
Yes. During onboarding, you can share documents (PDF, DOCX, PPTX) which our AI will use to better understand your company and improve mentor matching accuracy.

**How is my data handled?**
All company data is stored securely on Google Cloud (Firestore + GCS). It is used exclusively for mentor matching and program management within AlignCore. Data is not shared with third parties.

---

## After the Program

**What happens when the program ends?**
Mentor relationships can continue beyond the formal program at the discretion of both parties. We'll notify you one month before the formal program concludes.

**Can I reapply in a future cohort?**
Yes. If your company evolves significantly (new stage, pivoted direction), we encourage reapplication for re-matching with more relevant mentors.

**Can I refer another startup to AlignCore?**
Absolutely! Referrals are welcome. Ask your contact to email our inbox with their company details.
